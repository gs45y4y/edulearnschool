About the IC Portal

The Infinite Campus Portal provides students and parents with 24/7 access to real-time academic information. Stay connected with your student's educational progress, communicate with teachers, and manage important school-related tasks all in one convenient location.

For Students

  • View current grades and GPA
  • Check assignment due dates
  • Access daily schedule
  • Monitor attendance records
  • View test scores and progress reports
  • Update contact information
  • Register for courses

For Parents

  • Track student academic progress
  • View attendance and tardies
  • Monitor assignment completion
  • Receive automated notifications
  • Communicate with teachers
  • Update emergency contacts
  • Access report cards and transcripts

Additional Features

  • Mobile app access (iOS & Android)
  • Email and text notifications
  • Secure messaging with staff
  • Fee payments and lunch accounts
  • Calendar integration
  • Document uploads
  • Multi-student management

Getting Started

First Time Users

Step 1: Contact the school office at (719) 520-3382 to request your activation key.

Step 2: Visit the Infinite Campus portal and click "Create Account".

Step 3: Enter your activation key and follow the prompts to create your username and password.

Step 4: Set up your notification preferences and security questions.

Mobile App Access

Download the Infinite Campus Mobile Portal app from the App Store or Google Play Store.

iOS: Search "Infinite Campus Mobile Portal" in the App Store

Android: Search "Campus Parent" or "Campus Student" in Google Play

Use your existing portal credentials to log in on the mobile app.

Technical Requirements

Browsers: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge (latest versions recommended)

Mobile Devices: iOS 12+ or Android 8.0+

Internet: Stable internet connection required

Pop-ups: Enable pop-ups for the IC Portal domain

Privacy & Security

The Infinite Campus Portal uses industry-standard encryption to protect your information.

Tips: Never share your login credentials, log out when using shared computers, and change your password regularly.

Parents can only access information for their own students. Staff cannot access parent accounts.
