Translation Services

EduLearn K-12 is committed to ensuring all families have access to important school information in their preferred language. We offer several translation services to support our multilingual community.

Website Translation

Our website can be translated into over 100 languages using the Google Translate tool above. Simply select your language to view translated content.

Document Translation

Important documents such as enrollment forms, handbooks, and announcements are available in multiple languages. Contact the main office to request translated documents.

Interpretation Services

In-person and phone interpretation services are available for parent-teacher conferences, IEP meetings, and other school meetings. Please request an interpreter at least 48 hours in advance.

Multilingual Staff

Our district employs multilingual staff members who can assist families in Spanish, Chinese, Vietnamese, Tagalog, Hindi, and other languages.

How to Use Website Translation

Step 1: Select your preferred language from the dropdown menu above or click one of the quick language buttons.

Step 2: The page will automatically reload with translated content.

Step 3: Navigate through the website normally. All pages will be displayed in your selected language.

Step 4: To return to English, select "English" from the language menu.

Important Notes

  • Translation quality may vary depending on the complexity of the content
  • Some proper nouns, names, and technical terms may not be translated
  • PDFs and downloadable documents will remain in their original language unless specifically translated versions are available
  • For critical communications, please verify information with school staff
  • Translation features work best on updated web browsers
