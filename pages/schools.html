Our Schools

Discover the 15 exceptional schools that make up the EduLearn K-12 District family

EduLearn K-12 serves over 12,500 students across 15 schools in the Pleasanton area. Each school provides a safe, supportive learning environment with dedicated teachers and staff committed to student success. From early childhood through high school graduation, our schools prepare students for college, career, and life.
