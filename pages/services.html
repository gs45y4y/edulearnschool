Online Services

Access essential tools, resources, and services for students, parents, and staff

EduLearn K-12 provides a comprehensive suite of online services to support student learning and family engagement. Access grades, attendance, library resources, learning platforms, and more from any device, anytime.

Student Information & Grades

Monitor academic progress and school information

Infinite Campus Portal

Access real-time grades, attendance records, class schedules, and report cards. Parents can view information for multiple students and receive notifications about academic progress.

Access Portal

PowerSchool Learning

View assignments, submit homework, access course materials, and communicate with teachers. All coursework and resources organized by class period.

Access Learning

Google Classroom

Collaborate with classmates, access shared documents, submit assignments, and participate in class discussions using your school Google account.

Browse Catalog
